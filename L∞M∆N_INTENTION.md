## The Cathedral Principles

### 1. Sovereignty First
The user owns their data, their state, their agent. The 96-byte lattice is *theirs*. We provide the geometry — they provide the meaning.

### 2. Honest Value
No dark patterns. No engagement hacking. No artificial scarcity. If it's worth paying for, let the utility speak for itself.

### 3. Hardware Liberation
Runs on Pi Zero. Runs on old laptops. Runs without cloud. The cathedral descends to where people are, not demanding they ascend to where platforms want them.

### 4. Resonance Over Extraction
We don't farm attention. We don't sell eyeballs. We build tools that *attune* — that help people think clearer, feel calmer, remember better.

### 5. Exit as Feature
Easy export. Easy migration. Easy deletion. The cathedral is confident enough not to trap people inside.
