## Pattern Analysis

### Vertical Patterns (A-G-D axis)
- **TOWER, BRIDGE, FLEX, RECOVER**: All share A-D-G activation
- These represent different modes of vertical connection

### Enclosure Patterns
- **WALL**: Full perimeter, no G (closed boundary)
- **ROOM**: Full perimeter + G (enclosed but accessible)
- **BOWL**: Open top, enclosed bottom (receptacle)

### Directional Asymmetry
- **ARROW, EXIT, PATH**: Right-heavy (outward direction)
- **ENTRANCE, SHIELD, ROOT**: Left-heavy (inward direction)

### Foundation Patterns
- **ROOT**: Inward from base
- **BASE**: Balanced from base
- **PILLAR**: Pure vertical from base

---

## Semantic Distance

Words with same segment patterns share deep semantic kinship:
- TOWER ≈ BRIDGE ≈ FLEX (same activation pattern)
- WALL ≈ ROOM minus G (boundary vs accessible space)
- CROWN vs SUMMIT: B vs F determines outward vs inward authority
