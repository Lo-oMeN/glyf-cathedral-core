## Visualization Renaissance: From Constrained to Expansive

### The Critique That Changed Everything
**Ð≡ Light⁷'s feedback:** Visualizations were "lackluster" — jagged edges, pixel clipping, poor aesthetic appeal. The core requirement: they must be **captivating** to achieve intuitiveness and communicative clarity.

This was a wake-up call. Function is not enough. Beauty is not decoration — it's the primary interface through which understanding flows.

### Phase 1: The Beautiful Edition (`loom_transcendent.html`)
**28,165 bytes of visual soul.**

**Key improvements:**
- High DPI rendering for crisp edges on all displays
- Radial gradients instead of flat colors (gold → amber → deep space)
- Glow and shadow effects using `globalCompositeOperation`
- Particle trails that fade smoothly
- Screen blending mode for luminous interference patterns
- Anti-aliased precision throughout

**Visual elements:**
- Golden Vesica lens with pulsing center
- Flower of Life rendered with 7 overlapping circles
- Phyllotaxis spiral with 50+ golden dots at 137.507°
- Audio-reactive particle bursts on beats
- Beat flash overlay for strong pulses
